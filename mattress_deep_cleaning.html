Work flow   
step 1:   Power Vacuum

Our hi - tech power vacuum machine generates high frequency waves that vibrate the mattress surface which helps to effectively remove the dry waste like dust, dust mites, allergens, dry skin cell deposits, dirt and excrement even from the inner layers. The machine is equipped with a special HEPA filter which traps 99.97 per cent of everything it collects. IN THIS MACHINE YOU CAN SEE THE DUST REMOVED FROM YOUR SOFA.

step 2:   Applying Shampoo

Disinfectant shampoo is sprayed to melt the dirt. Since the shampoo is penetrating to the inner layer, it kills the germs, bacteria etc even in the inner layers also. This acts as a repellent and a retardant to dust mite reproduction. The shampoo we use is safe to pets and humans and is based on several natural ingredients.

step 3:   Manual scrubbing

The surface is then gently agitated manually to melt and remove the dirt fully.

step 4:   Rinse Extraction

Using high power shampoo extraction machine, the contaminated water and dirt is then extracted even from the inner layer. It rinse the surface with clear water and the proven spray-extraction method with the 2 bar spray pressure of the machine ensure the maximum deepest cleaning and leave only minimal residual moisture.

Note: Please note that we are not using any strong chemicals for mattress cleaning and hence any marks, stains or spots on mattress are not guaranteed to be removed. (Usage of such chemicals is banned by law since it causes serious skin diseases)

After The Process

Even though the shampoo is extracted back to its maximum, the sofa will be still wet and will take few hours for a complete drying. You need to keep them indoor with the A/C switched ON for proper and fast drying. We advise neither to keep the windows open nor to keep the mattress in outdoor for drying because the humidity in UAE climate doesn’t dry it properly, also it will attract more dust to it, which makes it again dirty.

Powerful combination of pressure, speed and temperature works together and the Microscopic steam particles force their way into even the hardest to reach crevices and tiniest recesses to thoroughly loosen dirt particles and is extracted by vacuuming. As steam cleaning does not use any chemical cleaning agents, it prevents allergen residues from forming on the cleaned surfaces leaving a safe surface for healthy living.
